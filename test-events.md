# Test Events

Example events to create when testing the app. Covers all four categories and a mix of capacities and dates.

---

## Academic

**Spring Research Symposium**

- Location: Petit Science Center, Room 210
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~10:00 AM
- Capacity: 80
- Description: Undergraduate and graduate students present their research projects across biology, chemistry, and computer science. Faculty judges award prizes in each division.

**Python for Data Science Workshop**

- Location: Classroom South, Computer Lab 402
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~2:00 PM
- Capacity: 30
- Description: Hands-on workshop covering pandas, matplotlib, and scikit-learn. Bring a laptop with Python 3.10+ installed. No prior data science experience required.

**Finals Study Jam**

- Location: University Library North, 2nd Floor Study Commons
- Date & Time: pick a date 1–2 weeks out, ~6:00 PM
- Capacity: 120
- Description: Open study session with free coffee and snacks. Tutors available for calculus, statistics, and writing. Quiet zones and collaboration zones available.

## Career

**Spring Career Fair**

- Location: Student Center East Ballroom
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~11:00 AM
- Capacity: 300
- Description: Over 40 companies recruiting for internships and full-time roles in tech, finance, and engineering. Bring printed resumes. Business casual dress recommended.

**Resume Review Drop-In**

- Location: University Career Services and Alumni Center, Room 120
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~1:00 PM
- Capacity: 25
- Description: One-on-one 15-minute resume reviews with career advisors. Walk-ins welcome. Bring a printed copy or have your resume open on your laptop.

**Tech Industry Panel: Breaking Into Software**

- Location: College of Law Building, Room 241
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~5:30 PM
- Capacity: 150
- Description: Four engineers from local startups and big tech companies share their career paths and answer questions about interviews, internships, and breaking into the industry.

## Social

**International Food Festival**

- Location: Hurt Park
- Date & Time: pick a weekend date, ~12:00 PM
- Capacity: 200
- Description: Student cultural organizations serve dishes from around the world. Live music, cooking demos, and a best-dish competition judged by the student body.

**Board Game Night**

- Location: Student Center West, 3rd Floor Lounge
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~7:00 PM
- Capacity: 40
- Description: Bring your favorite game or borrow from our library of 50+ titles. All skill levels welcome. Light snacks provided.

**End of Semester Rooftop Social**

- Location: Piedmont Central, Outdoor Courtyard
- Date & Time: pick a date near end of semester, ~6:00 PM
- Capacity: 60
- Description: Celebrate the end of the semester with music, lawn games, and a photo booth. Open to all students. Non-alcoholic drinks and snacks provided.

## Sports

**3-on-3 Basketball Tournament**

- Location: Student Recreation Center, Basketball Courts 1–4
- Date & Time: pick a weekend date, ~9:00 AM
- Capacity: 48
- Description: Single-elimination tournament. Register as a team of 3 or get matched with others. Prizes for first and second place. Sneakers required.

**Yoga on the Lawn**

- Location: Greenway at Hurt Park
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~8:00 AM
- Capacity: 50
- Description: Free outdoor yoga session led by a certified instructor. Bring a mat if you have one — extras available to borrow. All levels welcome.

**Intramural Soccer Sign-Up & Scrimmage**

- Location: Panthersville Intramural Fields, Field 2
- Date & Time: pick any upcoming date, ~4:00 PM
- Capacity: 22
- Description: Casual scrimmage for players interested in joining the intramural league. No experience necessary. Wear athletic clothes and cleats or sneakers.
